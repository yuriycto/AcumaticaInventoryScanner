2. About the Application

Acumatica Inventory Scanner is a mobile and desktop application that enables users to:

  • Scan product barcodes using the device's camera
  • Connect to their own Acumatica ERP system via secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Look up and view inventory item details from their Acumatica instance
  • View warehouse locations and stock quantities

Important: The App is a client-side tool that connects directly to YOUR Acumatica ERP system. We (AcuPower LTD) do not operate servers that store or process your business data.

4. How We Use Information

The information you provide is used exclusively to:

  1. Authenticate your connection to your Acumatica ERP system using industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol
  2. Query inventory data from your own Acumatica instance when you scan a barcode
  3. Display item details, warehouse locations, and stock information retrieved from your system
  4. Cache recently viewed items locally on your device for improved performance

We do not use your information for:

  • Marketing or advertising
  • Profiling or automated decision-making
  • Sale to third parties
  • Any purpose other than the App's core functionality

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage Architecture

All data remains on your device. The App uses a decentralized architecture where:

  • Configuration and credentials are stored locally using platform-native secure storage mechanisms
  • On Android: Android Keystore system
  • On iOS: iOS Keychain
  • On Windows/macOS: Platform-specific secure storage APIs

5.2 No Central Servers

AcuPower LTD does not operate servers that receive, store, or process your data. The App communicates directly between your device and your Acumatica instance. We have no access to:

  • Your Acumatica credentials
  • Your business data
  • Your inventory information
  • Any data you enter or view in the App

5.3 Security Measures

We have implemented comprehensive security measures to protect your personal data:

Security Feature Description
Encrypted Storage Credentials stored using platform-native encrypted secure storage
SSL/TLS Encryption All network communications use HTTPS encryption for secure data transfer
OAuth 2.0 Industry-standard authentication; passwords never stored
Token Expiration Access tokens expire after 1 hour, requiring re-authentication
No Data Transmission to Us Your data never passes through our systems
Strict Access Control Only you have access to your locally stored credentials
OWASP Standards App developed following OWASP security best practices
