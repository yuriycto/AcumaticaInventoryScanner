### Problem Statement
```
Managing inventory in a warehouse environment often requires quick access to item information, stock levels, and pricing details. While Acumatica ERP provides comprehensive inventory management through web interfaces, warehouse staff need a faster, more mobile-friendly way to look up items during daily operations. Carrying laptops or constantly returning to workstations isn't practical when you're on the warehouse floor.
```

### Solution Introduction
```
Acumatica Inventory Scanner solves this challenge by putting inventory lookup capabilities directly in your pocket. Simply scan a barcode with your phone's camera, and instantly see detailed item information from your Acumatica system‚Äîno need to type item codes or navigate complex menus.
```
