# Privacy Policy

**Acumatica Inventory Scanner**  
**Effective Date:** January 13, 2026  
**Developer:** AcuPower LTD

---

## Introduction

AcuPower LTD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Acumatica Inventory Scanner app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

The App collects the following information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Acumatica Instance URL** - The web address of your Acumatica ERP system
- **Username** - Your Acumatica login username
- **OAuth Client ID and Secret** - API credentials for authentication
- **API Version** - Your Acumatica API version

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Scanned Barcodes** - Barcode data scanned using the device camera (stored locally only)
- **Cached Inventory Data** - Recently viewed items cached on your device for offline access

### Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Camera** | Required to scan barcodes |
| **Internet** | Required to connect to your Acumatica instance |
| **Network State** | To check internet connectivity |

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. **Authenticate** with your Acumatica ERP system using OAuth 2.0
2. **Query** inventory data from your Acumatica instance
3. **Display** item details retrieved from your system
4. **Cache** recently viewed items for faster access

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

- All credentials and settings are stored **locally on your device**
- We do **NOT** transmit your credentials to any third-party servers
- We do **NOT** collect or store any data on our servers

### Security Measures

- Credentials are stored using platform-secure storage mechanisms
- All API communications use **HTTPS encryption**
- OAuth 2.0 tokens are used instead of storing passwords
- Tokens expire after 1 hour for additional security

---

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your data with any third parties.**

The App only communicates with:
- Your own Acumatica ERP instance (the URL you provide)

---

## Data Retention

- Settings and cached data remain on your device until you:
  - Uninstall the App
  - Use the "Reset All Settings" feature
  - Clear the app's data through device settings

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** - View all stored settings in the app
- **Delete** - Remove all data using "Reset All Settings"
- **Portability** - Settings are stored locally and can be cleared anytime

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
